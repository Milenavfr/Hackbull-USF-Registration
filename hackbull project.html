Making Every Course Count

"Securing a seat is only the first step. At USF CourseTrack, we understand that students need more than just availability—they need confidence in their academic choices. Once we alert you that a coveted spot has opened, you need to act fast. That’s why we encourage integrating quick access to professor reviews and ratings. Don’t just grab any seat; grab the right seat, backed by real student feedback, and register immediately."



How it works: Your course alert system

1. REAL-TIME TRACKING

Our lightweight poller script monitors the university registrar system every few minutes. We don't guess—we check the live status of every seat in your target section.

2. INSTANT NOTIFICATION

The moment a seat opens, our service instantly pushes an alert to your chosen contact method: SMS Text Message or Email. Speed is everything.

3. DECISION READY

We only notify, never auto-register. You receive the alert, confirm your choice (with reviews!), and register yourself, maintaining full control and security.
